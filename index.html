CHASING SUNSETS

Sept 13, 2016- Chasing Sunsets is one of my life themes. I'd love to say chasing sunrises it definitely seems more glamourous with its new beginnings and all the wonder of rising but honestly I just am not a morning person. Getting up that early hurts! Not actually but kind of. lol. However chasing sunsets has proved to work well and the beauty it evokes brings a certain clarity and hope for the new beginnings. No other thing makes me pause so reverently and just smile inside. Below are a few of the sunsets I have had the privilege to catch. Both above and below the clouds. Enjoy! -Stephanie Scales
